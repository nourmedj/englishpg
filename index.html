About us

We are a team of six students dedicated to helping first-year students at the National Higher School of Mathematics succeed in their studies. On our website, you’ll find valuable resources to support your academic journey, including:

  • Study plans tailored for each subject.
  • Techniques and tips to study efficiently.
  • Comprehensive summaries for each topic.
  • Course materials, TD sheets, exercises, and final exams.
  • A detailed timetable to help you organize your time effectively.
